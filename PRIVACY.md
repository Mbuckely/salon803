# Privacy Policy - Salon 803 Job Applications

**Effective Date:** January 20, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 20, 2025

## Introduction

Salon 803 ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide when applying for a position with us.

## Information We Collect

When you submit a job application through our website, we collect the following information:

### Required Information
- **Full Name:** To identify your application
- **Email Address:** For communication regarding your application
- **Phone Number:** For interview scheduling and communication
- **Position Applied For:** To match you with appropriate opportunities
- **Availability:** To assess scheduling compatibility
- **Professional Experience:** To evaluate your qualifications
- **Resume:** To review your work history and qualifications

### Automatically Collected Information
- **IP Address (Hashed):** Used for spam prevention and security monitoring. We do NOT store your actual IP addressâ€”only a cryptographic hash that cannot be reversed to identify you.
- **Browser Information (User Agent):** Used for technical troubleshooting and abuse detection.
- **Submission Timestamp:** To track when your application was received.

### Optional Information
- **Consent Status:** Record of your agreement to this privacy policy

## How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

1. **Application Review:** To evaluate your qualifications for employment
2. **Communication:** To contact you regarding your application status, interviews, or employment opportunities
3. **Compliance:** To maintain records as required by employment law
4. **Security:** To prevent spam, fraud, and abuse of our application system

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your information for marketing purposes without explicit consent
- Share your information with third parties except as described below

## Data Storage & Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Application Data:** Stored securely in Google Sheets with restricted access
- **Resume Files:** Stored in a private Google Drive folder, not publicly accessible
- **Access Control:** Only authorized Salon 803 personnel can access your information

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption:** Data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
- **Access Control:** Service accounts with minimal necessary permissions
- **Validation:** All data is validated to prevent malicious inputs
- **CAPTCHA:** Spam prevention to protect against automated abuse
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute-force attacks
- **No Public Access:** Your resume and personal information are never publicly accessible

### Data Retention

- **Active Applications:** Retained for 12 months from submission date
- **Hired Candidates:** Information transferred to employee records per employment law
- **Rejected Applications:** Deleted after 12 months unless you consent to be kept on file for future opportunities

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to process your application:

1. **Google Workspace (Sheets & Drive):**
   - Purpose: Secure storage of application data and resumes
   - Data Shared: All application information you provide
   - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. **hCaptcha:**
   - Purpose: Spam and bot prevention
   - Data Shared: Browser information, CAPTCHA interaction data
   - Privacy Policy: https://www.hcaptcha.com/privacy

3. **Resend (Optional):**
   - Purpose: Email notifications to internal team
   - Data Shared: Your name and position (not sent to you)
   - Privacy Policy: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

**Note:** These services have their own privacy policies. We carefully select partners who maintain strong privacy practices.

## Data Deletion Process

If you request deletion of your application:

1. We will verify your identity via email confirmation
2. We will delete your application data from Google Sheets
3. We will delete your resume file from Google Drive
4. We will send you confirmation once deletion is complete
5. Deletion is typically completed within 7 business days

**Note:** We may retain certain information if required by law (e.g., employment discrimination investigations).

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 16, please do not submit an application or provide any personal information.

## International Data Transfers

Your information is stored on servers located in the United States. If you are located outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data protection laws than your country.

By submitting an application, you consent to this transfer.

## Cookies & Tracking

Our application system does NOT use cookies for tracking purposes. The only data collection that occurs is:

- **CAPTCHA:** hCaptcha may use browser fingerprinting for spam detection
- **Form Submission:** Data you explicitly provide in the application form

We do NOT use analytics, advertising pixels, or other tracking technologies on the application page.
